Meet Neil Urbano

With over 15 years of automotive experience, Neil Urbano founded Urbz Mobile Auto Service with a simple mission: provide honest, professional auto repair that puts customers first.

What started as a mobile-only operation has grown into a full-service automotive solution, offering both the convenience of mobile service and the capabilities of a complete repair shop at 1045 Adelbert Ave in Stockton.

Neil's commitment to quality is evident in every repair. From sourcing lifetime-warranty parts from local dealers to completing complex jobs like manual clutch replacements in just 2 days, Urbz has built a reputation for excellence that customers trust.

But what really sets Urbz apart is the personal touch. Customers don't just mention the business in reviews - they mention Neil by name. That's the kind of relationship-driven service that's rare in today's auto repair industry.
