Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer mobile service?

Yes! We offer mobile service throughout the Stockton area. We can come to your home, workplace, or anywhere else that's convenient for you. Some complex repairs may require our shop facility.

What's your warranty policy?

We offer lifetime warranties on most parts sourced from local dealers, and 6 months warranty on labor. We stand behind our work and use quality components.

How quickly can you service my vehicle?

Most repairs are completed same-day or within 2 days. Complex jobs like clutch replacements typically take 2 days. We'll give you an accurate timeframe when you schedule.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for all services. Contact us with your vehicle information and the issue you're experiencing, and we'll give you an upfront quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and debit cards. Payment is due upon completion of service.

Do I need an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome at our shop, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure we can serve you promptly. Mobile service always requires advance scheduling.
